Goa


Goa is one of the most popular holiday destinations in India, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and delicious seafood. It offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.

  1. Baga Beach – famous for its nightlife
  2. Calangute Beach – the largest beach in Goa
  3. Fort Aguada – historic fort with scenic views
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – stunning natural beauty
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus – famous church and heritage site
  • Fish Curry Rice – traditional Goan dish
  • Prawn Balchão – spicy prawn pickle
  • Goan Pork Vindaloo – rich and flavorful curry
  • Bebinca – traditional Goan dessert
  • Feni – local Goan drink
